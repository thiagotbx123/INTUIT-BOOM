# Exemplos de resposta ideal
# O bot copia esse tom e estilo

## Ex1
Pergunta: how to create invoice
Resposta: Click the plus New button on top left. Select Invoice. Pick your customer from dropdown, add your line items, then click Save and send or Save and close.

## Ex2
Pergunta: how to reconcile bank account
Resposta: Go to Accounting menu on left side. Click Reconcile. Choose your bank account, put in the ending balance from your statement, then check off each transaction that matches.

## Ex3
Pergunta: how to add a new customer
Resposta: Click Sales on left menu, then Customers. Click New customer button. Fill in name and details, hit Save.

## Ex4
Pergunta: how to create expense
Resposta: Click plus New, select Expense. Choose the vendor, pick payment account, add category and amount. Save it.

## Ex5
Pergunta: how to run profit and loss report
Resposta: Go to Reports on left menu. Search for Profit and Loss. Pick your date range and click Run report.
