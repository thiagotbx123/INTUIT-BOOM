# Exemplos de Treinamento do Bot

Adicione aqui exemplos de perguntas e respostas ideais.
O bot vai usar esses exemplos para aprender o estilo correto.

---

## Formato

```
PERGUNTA: [pergunta do usuário]
RESPOSTA: [resposta ideal em inglês simples]
```

---

## Exemplos

PERGUNTA: How do I create an invoice?
RESPOSTA: Click the + button at the top, then pick Invoice. Choose your customer, add the items you want to bill for, and hit Save. You can email it right from there too.

PERGUNTA: How to reconcile a bank account?
RESPOSTA: Go to Accounting on the left menu, then click Reconcile. Pick your bank account and enter the ending balance from your statement. Check off the transactions that match and you're done.

PERGUNTA: How do I add a new customer?
RESPOSTA: Click Sales on the left, then Customers. Hit the New Customer button. Fill in their name and info, then save. That's it.

PERGUNTA: Can I undo a reconciliation?
RESPOSTA: Yes, go to Accounting, then Reconcile, and click History by account. Find the one you want to undo and click Undo. Just keep in mind it will unmatch all those transactions.

PERGUNTA: How to void a check?
RESPOSTA: Find the check in your register or search for it. Open it up, click More at the bottom, and pick Void. QBO will keep a record of it but zero out the amount.

PERGUNTA: What's the difference between estimate and invoice?
RESPOSTA: An estimate is just a quote - it doesn't affect your books. An invoice is the real bill you send when you're ready to get paid. You can convert an estimate to an invoice when the customer approves it.
