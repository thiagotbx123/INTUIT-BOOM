# TSA ONBOARDING PROJECT OVERVIEW
## Complete Corporate Onboarding Package

---

# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides a complete overview of the TSA (Technical Solutions Architect) Onboarding Program at TestBox. The program is designed to take a new hire from Day 0 to full autonomy in 90 days.

**Key Stats:**
- Duration: 90 days (formal onboarding)
- Time to basic productivity: 30 days
- Time to full autonomy: 90 days
- Buddy support: 30 days intensive

# DETAILED PHASE BREAKDOWN

## Phase 1: Pre-Boarding (-14 to Day 0)

### Manager Checklist
- [ ] Send welcome email (Day -14)
- [ ] Assign onboarding buddy
- [ ] Request IT accounts (Day -14)
- [ ] Order equipment (Day -14)
- [ ] Schedule Week 1 meetings (Day -7)
- [ ] Notify team (Day -7)
- [ ] Send credentials (Day -5)
- [ ] Send Week 1 calendar (Day -5)

### HR Checklist
- [ ] Send offer confirmation
- [ ] Send paperwork package (Day -12)
- [ ] Schedule benefits call
- [ ] Process paperwork (Day -7)
- [ ] Verify all access ready (Day -1)

### IT Checklist
- [ ] Create Google Workspace account
- [ ] Create Slack account
- [ ] Set up Zoom license
- [ ] Prepare equipment
- [ ] Ship equipment (Day -7)
- [ ] Verify equipment delivery (Day -2)

### Success Criteria
- All accounts active before Day 1
- Equipment received by new hire
- Week 1 calendar populated
- Buddy confirmed and notified

---

## Phase 2: Week 1 - Foundation (Days 1-5)

### Daily Themes
| Day | Theme | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 1 | Welcome | Culture, admin, team |
| 2 | Product | Platform deep dive |
| 3 | Customers | Market, accounts, Intuit |
| 4 | Technical | Architecture, security |
| 5 | Integration | Cross-team, reflection |

### Key Deliverables
- All access verified and working
- Platform 101 quiz passed
- Security awareness quiz passed
- Met all Solutions team members
- Week 1 reflection submitted
- Week 2 goals set

### Success Criteria
- New hire feels welcomed
- Basic product understanding
- Clear on first-month expectations
- Strong buddy relationship established

---

## Phase 3: Weeks 2-4 - Deep Dive & Practice

### Training Modules
| Module | Hours | Assessment |
|--------|-------|------------|
| Demo Environment Setup | 6 | Practical |
| Customer Discovery | 4 | Role-play |
| QBO Validation Process | 8 | Practical |
| Troubleshooting | 4 | Case study |
| Environment Health | 3 | Quiz |

### Shadow Sessions Required
- Minimum 5 customer interactions
- 1 discovery call
- 1 technical scoping
- 1 QBO validation
- 1 engineering handoff
- 1 demo delivery

### Hands-on Exercises
1. Environment Setup (supervised)
2. Discovery Call Simulation
3. QBO Feature Validation

### Success Criteria
- All training modules completed
- Shadow sessions documented
- First supervised tasks completed
- Positive feedback from supervisors

---

## Phase 4: Day 30 Checkpoint

### Review Meeting Agenda
1. Progress against milestones (15 min)
2. Self-assessment discussion (10 min)
3. Peer feedback review (10 min)
4. Goals for Day 60 (10 min)
5. Support needs (5 min)

### Milestones to Assess
**Knowledge:**
- [ ] Can explain TestBox value prop
- [ ] Knows top 10 customers
- [ ] Understands product suite
- [ ] Navigates tools confidently
- [ ] Understands QBO project

**Skills:**
- [ ] Sets up environments independently
- [ ] Handles basic customer questions
- [ ] Follows security protocols
- [ ] Uses Linear effectively

**Relationships:**
- [ ] Met all Solutions team
- [ ] Met key cross-functional contacts
- [ ] Strong buddy relationship

### Decision Points
- On track → Continue to Day 60 plan
- Behind → Adjust support and timeline
- Concerns → Early intervention

---

## Phase 5: Day 60 Checkpoint

### Review Meeting Agenda
1. Customer interaction quality (15 min)
2. Technical skills assessment (15 min)
3. Peer feedback synthesis (10 min)
4. Career development discussion (15 min)
5. Day 90 stretch goals (5 min)

### Milestones to Assess
**Knowledge:**
- [ ] Deep understanding of 3 accounts
- [ ] Knows competitor landscape
- [ ] Understands product roadmap
- [ ] Mastered QBO validation

**Skills:**
- [ ] Leads full discovery calls
- [ ] Configures complex scenarios
- [ ] Troubleshoots independently
- [ ] Creates customer documentation

**Relationships:**
- [ ] Primary contact for 1+ customer
- [ ] Regular engineering collaboration
- [ ] Contributing to team discussions

---

## Phase 6: Day 90 Checkpoint

### Review Meeting Agenda
1. Comprehensive performance review (20 min)
2. Goal achievement assessment (15 min)
3. Transition from onboarding (10 min)
4. Career planning (10 min)
5. Feedback on onboarding program (5 min)

### Final Assessment Areas
- Product expertise
- Customer knowledge
- Technical execution
- Communication
- Collaboration
- Problem-solving

### Outcomes
- Transition to normal management cadence
- Clear development goals for next quarter
- Feedback captured for program improvement
- New hire is fully integrated team member

# RISK MANAGEMENT

## Common Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Access not ready Day 1 | Medium | High | IT checklist, Day -2 verification |
| Buddy unavailable | Medium | Medium | Backup buddy assigned |
| Information overload | High | Medium | Paced schedule, reflection time |
| Isolation (remote) | Medium | High | Daily buddy contact, team events |
| Manager too busy | Medium | High | Delegated touchpoints to buddy |
| Training gaps | Low | Medium | Feedback loops, adjustment |

## Escalation Path
1. New Hire → Buddy (daily issues)
2. Buddy → Manager (patterns/concerns)
3. Manager → HR (program issues)
4. HR → Leadership (systemic problems)
